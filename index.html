Biography

Li is a researcher in Computer Science at the Hong Kong Polytechnic University, under the supervision of Prof. Daniel Xiapu Luo. Previously, he received his M.Sc in Information Security from Chinese Academy of Sciences, and B.Sc in Information Security from Central South University. His current research interests include Blockchain/Mobile/System Security and Privacy, Ethereum/Smart Contract, Software Engineering, and Static/Dynamic Program Analysis.​ He received best paper awards from INFOCOM'18, ISPEC'17, CCF'18, and outstanding reviewer award from FGCS'17. He also received student grants from OSDI'20 and USENIX SEC'20.
